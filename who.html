Meet Our Managing Director

Jamie Judge - Managing Director

Jamie Judge has a long history in the aviation industry. Starting as an apprentice aircraft mechanic in SRS Aviation, Shannon in 1998, Jamie gained deep insight into commercial airliners by working on some of the most complex structural repairs, regularly performed on Boeing 727’s built in the 1960s.

After completing his apprenticeship, Jamie specialized in structural repair work as a contractor for a year. In 2003, he joined Cityjet as a Flight Line Aircraft Engineer, where he applied his skills with an EASA Part 66 B1 license for 9 years, based primarily at Charles De Gaulle Airport in France. After moving home in 2012, Jamie transitioned to the leased aircraft transition management field as a technical consultant, managing multiple aircraft transitions and overseeing on-site teams. His expertise in managing large-scale projects is unparalleled in the industry.

Jamie then joined GECAS, the world’s largest aircraft lessor, gaining valuable experience in the financial, commercial, and technical aspects of aircraft leasing. In 2021, he began lecturing at Limerick Institute of Technology, sharing his wealth of experience in aircraft transition management.

With his profound experience, Jamie now leads the ALS team in Shannon, bringing a sustainable aviation future through the power of digitalisation.

In his current role, Jamie oversees the seamless transition from physical aircraft records to fully digitalized solutions, providing clients with the security and ease of access that modern aviation demands. With his hands-on approach and dedication to innovation, Jamie ensures that ALS remains at the forefront of aviation logistics, offering state-of-the-art solutions for clients worldwide.

Jamie’s mission is to continue advancing lean process management within the aviation sector, ensuring that each step is efficient, cost-effective, and environmentally sustainable, contributing to the future of the industry.
